Our Story

King Lottery was founded in 2020 with a simple but powerful mission: to create India's most transparent, secure, and rewarding lottery platform. We saw a market full of distrust and opacity — and we set out to change that.

Starting with just 200 members in Mumbai, we have grown into a platform trusted by over 2.5 lakh players across 28 states. Every draw is conducted with full transparency, and every rupee won is paid within 24 hours.

Today, King Lottery stands as the symbol of integrity in India's lottery industry — where the crown belongs to every honest player who plays fair and wins big.
